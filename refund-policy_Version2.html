Refund & Return Policy

At Marie Custom Goods, customer satisfaction is our priority. Please read our refund and return policy below:

1. Returns

  • Returns are accepted only for items that are defective, damaged, or incorrect upon delivery.
  • To be eligible for a return, you must contact us within 3 days of receiving your item.
  • Items must be unused, in their original condition, and with original packaging.
  • Customized or personalized items are not eligible for return unless defective or incorrect.

4. How to Start a Return
